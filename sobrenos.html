About Avenged Sevenfold

Avenged Sevenfold is an american heavy metal band formed in Huntington Beach, California, in 1999. The bands' name comes from a passage in the Bible's Book of Genesis, adding an enigmatic and mystic dimension to their identity. Known for their iconic and diverse sound A7X have developed a devoted fanbase throughout the years.

Their live performances are stunning where they focus on having the best live sound possible (which they deliver). Avenged Sevenfold's live performances are a high-octane spectacle, characterized by M. Shadows' dynamic vocals, Synyster Gates' virtuosic guitar solos, and elaborate stage setups featuring pyrotechnics and dramatic lighting. Their commitment to delivering an electrifying show has earned them a reputation as one of the most thrilling live acts in the rock and metal scene, leaving fans with unforgettable memories after each concert. Theor most famous cocnert is "Live in the LBC" which was recorder as a DVD and later released as a live album. "Live in the LBC" is considered by many the band's best live performance ever.

The bands discography consists of 8 studio albums, a compilation album and a live album.. "City of Evil" (2005) sold over 2 million copies worldwide, while their self-titled album "Avenged Sevenfold" (2007) achieved significant commercial success. "Nightmare" (2010) debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 and was certified platinum. "Hail to the King" (2013) continued their streak with a number one debut on the Billboard 200 and certified gold status.
